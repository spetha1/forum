Harbor Seals Around The World

Meet infamous Harbor Seals from around the world!

 

Fun Facts!

While there are many seal colonies scattered around the globe - there are many specific harbor seals within aquarium facilities that have garnered specific internet attraction! These seals stand out due to their special qualities - or their undeniable cute factor. Some seals, such as Skinny, are known for breaking records (such as being the longest living seal on record). Other seals, such as Mizore-Chan, were known for their loveable, round charm.
